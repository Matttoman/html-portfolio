The Best Movies According to Matthew

My top 3 movies of all time.

3rd - Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 2

This is the final installment in the Harry Potter series. The climax of the saga brings us on an unexpected journey to a expected conclusion, it ended the only way it could, but the journey was fun.

2nd - Harry Potter and the Half Blood Prince

This was the first of the series that showed the characters less as children with child issues but instead like they had a stake in the fight agains Voldomort.

1st - Harry Potter and the Deathly Hallows Part 1

This was the literal coming of age for Harry, as he turned 17 and no longer underage for practicing magic. This was a pivital moment and distinction for the remainder of the series as he got closer and closer in the hunt for Voldomort.
